What's inside

Five analytical lenses
on NYC taxi data

Overview Dashboard
KPI cards, hourly and daily trip volumes, vendor comparison, passenger distribution, and monthly trends; all precomputed at startup for instant load.
Time Pattern Analysis
Hourly fare and speed curves reveal when the city is fastest, most expensive, and most active. Multi-dataset day-of-week breakdown included.
Trip Explorer
Paginated, filterable table of all trips. Filter by hour, day, vendor. Sort any column. Server-side pagination keeps responses fast.
Geo Pickup Map
Canvas scatter plot of up to 500 pickup coordinates, colored by speed. Includes a distance-vs-fare scatter and hour density chart.
Anomaly Detection
Custom Z-score algorithm (no libraries) identifies statistical outliers across duration, distance, speed, and fare. Flags trips where |z| > 2.5.
